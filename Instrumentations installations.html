MULTI-CARRIER SYSTEM MCS

Its configurable linear transport rail can be easily integrated into existing intralogistics and standard conveyor systems, with precise synchronization and seamless infeed and out feed of transport carriers. Movement of carriers within the system can be rapidly adapted to deal with different formats, sizes and types of product – down to batch sizes of one, or even to handle seasonal requirements.
