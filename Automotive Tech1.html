Driver drowsiness detection(Fatigue Management Systems) are essential car safety technology designed to prevent accidents caused by the driver becoming tired. This essential technology assists in preventing accidents by monitoring the driver or operator of a vehicle or heavy duty machinery alertness or response. Ways in which the FMS does its job is by monitoring the steering pattern monitoring, which relies on data from the electric power steering system to assess whether the driver is actively steering or becoming less responsive. Another method involves vehicle position in lane monitoring, which uses a camera to ensure the vehicle stays within lane markings, a task that can become challenging as a driver's alertness wanes. Other ways involves a camera with body heat sensors that will monitor the driver or operator’s eyes, head movement to gain an overview of the driver or operator’s physical state. Advantages
